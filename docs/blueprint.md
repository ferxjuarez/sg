# **App Name**: Machado Automotive Excellence

## Core Features:

- Service Showcase: Admin-managed gallery for showcasing completed repair and polishing work through videos and images, highlighting service quality.
- Technician Bio: Display a brief professional biography of the technician, emphasizing expertise and experience.
- Automated Quotation Tool: Enable users to upload photos and descriptions of required work for an initial estimate. Enhance quotation accuracy by adding a feature powered by generative AI that recommends a more accurate initial quote, suggesting relevant services based on visual analysis of uploaded images. This acts as a tool, assisting staff, and reducing initial inaccuracies or the back and forth in finalizing accurate quotes.
- Database Integration: Store user-submitted quote requests and admin-uploaded showcase content in a Supabase database for efficient management and retrieval.
- Contact Form: Allow potential customers to reach out through a form for inquiries and appointments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep grey (#333333), reflecting the premium feel of McLaren's aesthetics.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F0F0), providing a neutral base to make the content stand out.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF), drawing attention to key interactive elements and mirroring high-performance styling.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, known for its clean and versatile appearance, suitable for body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, offering a modern, technical look appropriate for headlines and short blocks of text; for longer text, use 'Inter' for body.
- Use a set of streamlined icons in electric blue to guide users and add visual interest.
